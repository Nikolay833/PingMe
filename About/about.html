Hack TUES 12 — Built in 28 hours

Preserving knowledge,
one ping at a time

PingMe connects curious travelers with Bulgaria's last living carriers of irreplaceable local knowledge — before it disappears forever.

Bulgaria's elderly knowledge carriers — herbalists, craftspeople, storytellers, and local legends — hold traditions that no book has ever captured. PingMe exists to make those connections happen before time runs out.

What we built

Features so far

Everything you need to discover, connect with, and learn from a local legend.

🗺️

Live Map

Explore knowledge carriers across Bulgaria on an interactive map.

🤖

AI Match

Get matched to the perfect legend based on your interests using vector embeddings.

📅

Book Sessions

Reserve time with a local legend directly online in seconds.

⏳

Extinction Timer

See how many sessions remain before a legend's knowledge is lost forever.

📜

Knowledge Docs

AI-preserved documents that capture and archive each legend's knowledge.

🎫

Experience Passport

Collect stamps from every session you complete on your journey.
